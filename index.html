About the Studio

BUILT FOR
YOUR VISION

Nova Lens was founded on a single conviction: every model deserves imagery that truly reflects who they are. Since 2012, our studio has partnered with emerging and established talent across fashion, beauty, and commercial photography.

We work closely with modeling agencies across New York and beyond to develop talent portfolios that convert — digitals, comp cards, and editorial sets that get you signed and booked.

📷

Editorial Excellence

Every session is treated as an editorial story — thoughtfully lit, deliberately composed, and retouched to industry standard.

🤝

Model-First Approach

We work at your pace. Our sets are collaborative, relaxed spaces designed to bring out your most natural, compelling self.

✦

Agency-Ready Results

We know what agencies and casting directors look for. Every deliverable is formatted and styled to open doors, not just fill drives.
